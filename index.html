Got Questions? We've
Got Answers!

What materials are your Sofa made of?

Our sofas are made with durable wooden or metal frames, high-density foam for comfort, and a variety of premium upholstery options, including fabric, leather, and microfiber. We use high-quality, sustainable materials to ensure both longevity and style. The cushions are designed for support and comfort, while the fabrics are selected for their texture, durability, and ease of maintenance. Whether you're looking for a soft, cozy fabric or a sleek, modern leather finish, we have options to suit every preference.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy, provided the sofa is unused, in its original condition, and with the original packaging. To initiate a return or exchange, simply contact our customer support team, and they will guide you through the process. Please note that shipping costs may be applicable for returns, and items must be returned within the specified timeframe. Terms and conditions may apply, and certain exclusions may apply for custom-made or final sale items.

How do I choose the right Sofas for my space?

To choose the right sofa for your space, start by measuring the dimensions of your room to ensure a proper fit. Consider your preferred style, color, and material to complement your decor. Think about the layout and functionality, such as the number of seats needed or whether you prefer a sectional or a standard sofa. Also, evaluate factors like comfort, durability, and ease of maintenance to find a sofa that suits your lifestyle and space requirements.

What is your deliver process?

Our delivery process starts with order confirmation and scheduling a convenient delivery date. On the scheduled day, our professional team will deliver the sofa to your specified location. We ensure safe handling during transit and careful placement in your space. Assembly and setup services are available upon request to make the process seamless. We’ll also remove packaging materials, leaving your space clean and ready to enjoy your new sofa.

How do I contact customer support for assistance?

You can contact our customer support team through email, phone, or the "Contact Us" page on our website. For immediate assistance, you can call our dedicated support line. Alternatively, you can fill out the form on the "Contact Us" page, and we will respond promptly. Our team is available during business hours to assist with product information, order tracking, returns, exchanges, and any other inquiries you may have.
